Rummikub

  • A family game of strategy that’s never the same!
  • Played with 106 tiles numbered 1-13 in four different colors.
  • The object of the game is to go ‘Rummikub’ being the first player to place all the tiles from their rack onto the table.
  • Box size: 26.7x40x5.2 cm
